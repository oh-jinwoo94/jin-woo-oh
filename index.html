Jin Woo Oh

Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering, Johns Hopkins | Scientist, Lunit

I develop algorithms and computational models to decode how genomes encode, regulate, and evolve biological function.

My research spans regulatory genomics and spatial biology, seeking to link sequence-level determinants of regulation to spatially organized multicellular systems.

At Lunit, I apply these approaches to spatial oncology, modeling the tumor microenvironment to understand mechanisms of therapeutic response and resistance.
